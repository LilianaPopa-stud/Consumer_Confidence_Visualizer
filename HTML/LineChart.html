Selected Chart Type: Line Chart

The Line Chart visualizes the Consumer Confidence Index (CCI) for a specific time period across multiple selected countries. The CCI represents the economic outlook and consumer sentiment within each country. This interactive chart allows users to compare and analyze the CCI trends over time, providing insights into the confidence levels of consumers in different countries. By selecting specific countries, users can focus on the CCI variations and identify potential patterns or correlations.
